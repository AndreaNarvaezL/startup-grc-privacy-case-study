# GRC & Data Protection Case Study for a Digital AgriTech Startup

This repository presents a practical case study focused on the legal and compliance structuring of a digital application in the agricultural sector.

The objective is to demonstrate how Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC), data protection, and information security principles can be applied in the development of a digital product.

---

## Objective

To design a compliance and risk management framework for a startup application, including:

- Identification and assessment of legal and operational risks  
- Data protection and privacy considerations  
- Incident response from a legal perspective  
- Compliance documentation and controls  

---

## Scope

This case study study a startup that:

- Collects user data (farmers, clients, transactions)  
- Operates through a digital platform  
- Requires compliance with data protection and regulatory frameworks  

---

## Key Areas Covered

- Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)  
- Data Protection & Privacy  
- Regulatory Compliance  
- Risk Assessment & Mitigation  
- Information Security (legal perspective)  
- Incident Response  

---

## Repository Structure

- risk-matrix.md` → Identification and evaluation of risks  
- data-flow-assessment.md` → Data lifecycle and privacy risks  
- incident-response-protocol.md` → Legal approach to security incidents  
- privacy-policy-outline.md` → Structure of privacy compliance  
- compliance-checklist.md` → Key controls and requirements  

---

## Approach

This project follows a **risk-based approach**, integrating:

- Preventive compliance  
- Privacy by Design principles  
- Legal risk management in digital environments  

---

## Key Insight

Legal compliance is not only reactive — it must be integrated into the design and development of digital products.
