# Risk Matrix | AgriTech Digital Application

This document presents a risk assessment for a digital application in the agricultural sector, focusing on legal, regulatory, and information security risks.

---

## Objective

To identify, evaluate, and propose mitigation strategies for key risks associated with the operation of a digital platform handling user data and transactions.

---

## Risk Assessment Criteria

- **Likelihood (L):** Low / Medium / High  
- **Impact (I):** Low / Medium / High  
- **Risk Level:** Based on combined likelihood and impact  

---

## Risk Matrix

| # | Risk Description | Category | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Level | Mitigation Strategy |
|--|-----------------|----------|-----------|--------|-----------|--------------------|
| 1 | Unauthorized access to user data | Information Security | High | High | Critical | Implement access controls, authentication mechanisms, and encryption |
| 2 | Collection of personal data without proper consent | Compliance / Data Privacy | Medium | High | High | Implement clear consent mechanisms and privacy notices |
| 3 | Data breach affecting sensitive information | Information Security / Legal | Medium | High | High | Incident response plan and breach notification procedures |
| 4 | Lack of privacy policy or unclear data usage | Compliance | Medium | Medium | Medium | Develop and publish privacy policy aligned with regulations |
| 5 | Inadequate data retention practices | Compliance / Data Governance | Medium | Medium | Medium | Define data retention and deletion policies |
| 6 | Vulnerabilities in input fields (XSS, injection) | Application Security | High | Medium | High | Input validation and secure coding practices |
| 7 | Third-party service risks (APIs, integrations) | Operational / Compliance | Medium | High | High | Vendor risk assessment and contractual controls |
| 8 | Misuse of user data for unintended purposes | Legal / Ethical | Low | High | Medium | Data usage limitations and internal controls |
| 9 | Lack of incident response protocol | Governance | Medium | High | High | Develop and implement incident response procedures |
|10 | Regulatory non-compliance (data protection laws) | Compliance | Medium | High | High | Continuous compliance monitoring and legal review |

---

## Key Observations

- The most critical risks are related to **data protection and unauthorized access**
- Compliance risks are closely linked to **lack of proper documentation and controls**
- Security risks directly impact legal exposure and reputational damage

---

## Risk Management Approach

This assessment follows a **risk-based approach**, prioritizing:

- Prevention through proper design (Privacy by Design)
- Implementation of controls
- Continuous monitoring and improvement

---

## Conclusion

Effective risk management in digital products requires integration between legal, technical, and operational perspectives, ensuring compliance and protection of user data.
